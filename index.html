Meet Your Barber

Aarav Khanna

Founder & Hair Artist

Aarav built The Gilded Chair from a single conviction — that a great haircut is more than a service, it's a ritual. Trained in precision cutting and classic barbering techniques, he brings an artist's eye and a craftsman's hands to every visit.

Whether it's a sharp fade, a textured crop, or a clean straight-razor shave, each cut is approached with the same level of care — detailed, intentional, personal.
